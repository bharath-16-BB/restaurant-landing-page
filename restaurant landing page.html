About Us

Welcome to Packet Hotel, Guntur's premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine. Established in 1995, we have been serving generations of food lovers with our traditional recipes passed down through the ages.

Our chefs use only the freshest local ingredients combined with exotic spices to create dishes that are a perfect balance of flavor, aroma, and texture. From fiery Andhra specialties to rich Mughlai delicacies, our menu offers something for every palate.

At Packet Hotel, we believe dining is not just about food but about creating memorable experiences. Our warm hospitality and elegant ambiance make every visit special.
